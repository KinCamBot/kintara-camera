KinCam Cinematic Camera

First-person, free-cam photos, over-the-shoulder, and one-click HYPE reels, right inside Kintara.

Install in about a minute

1
Download KinCam and unzip it. Right-click the file and choose Extract All, then remember where the folder lands (your Downloads folder is fine). ⬇  Download KinCam
2
Open the extensions page. Copy the address below, paste it into your browser's address bar, and press Enter:
click to copy
Works in Chrome, Edge and Brave. Brave uses brave://extensions, Edge uses edge://extensions, but the copy button works in all of them.
3
Turn on Developer mode. Flip the toggle in the top-right corner of that page:
4
Load unpacked. Three buttons appear once Developer mode is on. Click Load unpacked, then pick the KinCam folder you unzipped in step 1: Pick the folder itself, the one that has manifest.json inside it. Not the zip.
5
Open kintara.gg/play and log in. The KinCam panel appears at the top-right. Click the KinCam icon in your toolbar any time to show or hide it. 🎥
